Day 1


Yangon


Discover the symbolic Shwedagon Pagoda – the pride of Myanmar & Burmese people.

Day 2


Mandalay


Discover Mandalay – the cultural capital of Burma(Walk on the world’s longest teak bridge: U Bein Bridge,explore the Mandalay palace.

Day 3


Pyin-Oo-Lwin


Gaze upon the beauty of the National Kandawgyi Botanical Gardens containing 514 species of indigenous trees and 74 foreign species, as well as 300 types of orchids and 25 different roses.

Day 4


Taung Gyi


The pine covered hill station of Taunggyi provides a cool break from the heat of the plains. Due to its function as a trade town from Thailand, China, and India, it is one of the most prosperous and enterprising towns in Myanmar.

Day 5


Yangon


Admire one of the grandest Buddhist pagodas in the world: Shwedagon Pagoda.
